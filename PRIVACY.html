Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and have therefore adopted these privacy guidelines to govern the relationship between you and Us. 

What information do we collect? 
We may collect personal information from you to provide you with products, services, and information and to notify you about new products and services we think you will find interesting. If at any stage, you no longer wish to receive these notifications please notify us by e-mail at a1tradies.co.nz@gmail.com.

HOW I GATHER INFORMATION 
We gather information by you disclosing personal information on this website using on-line form & Get in Touch application forms, and when you send us an e-mail. 

We may collect the following type of information:
Your name
Your e-mail addresses
Your address 
Your Contact Details
Other information which you provide to us related to your query

WHAT WE WILL NOT DO 
We will not under any circumstances sell, publish, or give away information to others unless the law requires us to. We may provide aggregate statistics about our customers; however, these statistics will not have any personal information which would identify you. 

Only specific employees of A1Tradies are able to access your personal data This policy means that we may require proof of identity before we disclose any information to you Changing, Correcting and Updating Information.
You have the right at all times to access, amend or update your personal details. If you do not want to receive any further communications from A1Tradies please send us an email at email address a1tradies.co.nz@gmail.com. 

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY 
We respect your privacy and will notify you by posting any changes on this website in respect of our privacy policy so that you are aware of how we are treating your personal information. Please feel free to contact us by email if you have any comments or questions about our privacy policy at a1tradies.co.nz@gmail.com.
