General Repairs

Fixing minor electrical, plumbing, or carpentry issues around the home.      Repairing doors, windows, cabinets, and other fixtures.

Painting & Touch-Ups

Interior and exterior painting, including touch-ups and repainting of walls, ceilings, and trim. 
Staining or painting fences, decks, and other outdoor structures.

Carpentry Services

Building or repairing shelves, cabinets, furniture, or other wooden structures. ·
Installing or repairing molding, trim, and baseboards.

Plumbing Repairs

Fixing leaky faucets, repairing or replacing pipes, and addressing minor plumbing issues.         Installing or replacing fixtures like sinks, faucets, or toilets.

Electrical Repairs

Addressing minor electrical problems such as faulty switches, outlets, or lighting fixtures.         Installing or replacing light fixtures, ceiling fans, or electrical outlets.

Drywall Repair

Patching and repairing holes, cracks, or dents in drywall.         Taping, mudding, and sanding for a smooth finish.

Door & Window Repairs

Fixing squeaky doors, adjusting or replacing door hinges.         Repairing or replacing window frames, screens, or glass.

Flooring Repairs

Fixing damaged tiles, floorboards, or vinyl flooring.         
Repairing or replacing grout or caulk in bathrooms and kitchens.

Appliance Installation and Repair

Installing or replacing appliances such as dishwashers, ovens, or refrigerators.          Addressing minor issues with household appliances.

Gutter Maintenance

Cleaning gutters, removing debris, and repairing minor issues to ensure proper drainage.        Installing gutter guards for preventive maintenance.

Pressure Washing

Cleaning exterior surfaces, such as sidewalks, decks, driveways, and siding, using high-pressure water. · Preparing surfaces for painting or staining.

Fence & Gate Repairs

Repairing or replacing damaged fence panels or gates.          Staining or painting wooden fences for preservation.

Minor HVAC Repairs

Changing air filters, cleaning vents, and addressing minor heating or cooling system issues. · Installing or replacing thermostats.

Weather Stripping

Installing or replacing weather stripping around doors and windows to improve energy efficiency.

Assembling Furniture

Assembling and installing furniture, shelving units, or other items. ·         Mounting TVs, mirrors, or artwork.
