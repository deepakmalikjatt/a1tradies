Residential Carpentry

Residential carpentry involves the construction, installation, and repair of wooden structures and components within residential buildings.

1. Framework: Building frameworks for houses, including walls, floors, and roofs.
2. Interior Finishing: Installing doors, windows, trim, and molding.
3. Cabinetry: Constructing and installing kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, and other built-in furniture.
4. Flooring: Installing hardwood, laminate, tile, or other types of flooring.
5. Decking and Fencing: Building decks, porches, and fences around residential properties.

Industrial Carpentry

Industrial carpentry encompasses a range of specialized services focused on construction, installation, maintenance, and repair within industrial settings.

1. Structural Construction:- Heavy-duty frames, Heavy timber
2. Maintenance & Repair:- Machinery mounts, equipment platforms to ensure operational efficiency & safety.
3. Specialized Fabrication:- Industrial-grade shelving, storage racks, and material handling systems.
4. Safety Features:- Installing safety barriers, guardrails, and access platforms
5. Constructing custom workbenches, tool storage cabinets, and ergonomic workstations tailored to industrial needs.

Carpentry Services

General Carpentry Services

1. Framing: Building the structure of buildings, including walls, floors, and roofs.
2. Structural Repairs: Fixing damaged or weakened structural elements.
3. Studwork: Installing studs and framing for walls and partitions.
4. Joist and Beam Installation: Installing floor joists and beams for structural support.

Interior Carpentry

1. Trim Carpentry: Installing baseboards, crown molding, chair rails, and other decorative moldings.
2. Door Installation and Repair: Hanging and fitting interior and exterior doors.
3. Window Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing windows and window frames.
4. Built-in Furniture: Designing and building custom shelving, cabinets, and storage units.

Cabinetry and Furniture

1. Kitchen Cabinets: Designing, building, and installing custom kitchen cabinetry.
2. Bathroom Vanities: Creating and installing custom bathroom storage solutions.
3. Custom Furniture: Crafting bespoke furniture pieces such as tables, chairs, and beds.

Exterior Carpentry

1. Deck Construction and Repair: Building and repairing decks, porches, and outdoor stairs.
2. Fence Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing wooden fences and gates.
3. Siding Installation and Repair: Installing and replacing exterior siding materials.

Specialized Carpentry

2. Fireplace Mantels: Designing and installing custom fireplace surrounds and mantels.
3. Wine Cellar Construction: Building specialized storage areas for wine collections.

Wood Flooring

Restoration and Preservation

Custom Woodworking

Framing

Carpenters are involved in framing structures for buildings, which includes constructing the skeleton or framework using wooden studs, beams, and other components.

Installation

Carpenters install doors, windows, cabinets, and other wooden fixtures in homes and commercial buildings. They ensure proper fit, function, and aesthetic appeal.

Finish Carpentry

This involves the final touches in construction, such as installing trim, molding, baseboards, and other decorative elements. Finish carpenters focus on enhancing the visual appeal of a space.

Custom Furniture

Carpenters can create bespoke furniture pieces, including tables, chairs, cabinets, and more, tailored to the specific needs and preferences of clients.

Cabinet Making

Carpenters specializing in cabinetry design and build custom cabinets for kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and other spaces. 

Repairs & Renovations

Carpenters are often called upon to repair or renovate existing structures. This may include fixing damaged wood, replacing worn-out components, or upgrading features.

Deck & Fence Construction

Carpenters can design and build outdoor structures such as decks and fences, adding functionality and aesthetic appeal to residential or commercial properties.

Wooden Flooring Installation

Carpenters install various types of wooden flooring, including hardwood, laminate, or engineered wood, to enhance the interior of buildings.

Staircase Construction

Carpenters can design and build staircases for both interior and exterior spaces, ensuring they meet safety standards and complement the overall design.

Shelving & Storage Solutions

Carpenters can create custom shelving units and storage solutions to optimize space and meet the specific storage needs of clients. 

Restoration Work

Carpenters with expertise in historical preservation may be involved in restoring and preserving old wooden structures, maintaining their original aesthetic while ensuring structural integrity.
