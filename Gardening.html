Gardening services encompass a variety of tasks aimed at maintaining and enhancing outdoor spaces, from residential gardens to commercial landscapes and public areas.

Lawn Mowing

A lawn mowing service typically refers to a professional service that specializes in cutting grass and maintaining lawns.

1. Mowing: Regularly cutting the grass to a specified height.
2. Edging: Trimming the edges of the lawn along sidewalks, driveways, and flower beds.
3. Weeding: Removing weeds from the lawn and garden areas.
4. Fertilizing: Applying fertilizers to promote healthy grass growth.
5. Aeration: Loosening soil to allow air, water, and nutrients to penetrate the grass roots.
6. Seeding and sodding: Planting new grass seeds or laying sod to fill in bare spots.
